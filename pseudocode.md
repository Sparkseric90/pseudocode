**START**: How to wash your hands:

```
1. Turn on water to desired temp;
2. Dispense desired amount of soap into hands;
3. Rub Hands together for 30 seconds in running water;
    a. If hands are not clean then repeat step 3;
    b. If hands are clean then proceed to next step;
4. Rinse hands of soap;
5. Turn water off;
6. Use towels to dry hands off;
    a. If no towels THEN use blow dryer until wet hands are dry;
```

**End**: End program
